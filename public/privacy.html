Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025

Summary: We prioritize your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our service, never sell your data, and give you full control over your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Claims submitted for verification (used to improve our service)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, and IP address for security

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve our fact-checking services
  • Process your verification requests
  • Send important service updates (with your consent)
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse

3. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Verification history: 90 days by default (configurable)
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics) under strict agreements
  • Law enforcement when legally required

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Authentication (essential)
  • Preferences (functional)
  • Analytics (optional, privacy-respecting)

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes via email or prominent notice on our website.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at:

privacy@verity.systems
