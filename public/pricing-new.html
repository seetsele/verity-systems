Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Verity pricing

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! The Free tier gives you 10 verifications per day forever. Pro and Team plans include a 7-day free trial with full access.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

You'll receive a notification and can either upgrade your plan or wait until the next billing cycle. We never charge overage fees without consent.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! No contracts, no commitments. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Is there a discount for nonprofits?

Yes! Journalists and nonprofit organizations get 40% off any plan. Contact us with verification of your status.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice or wire transfer.

How does the private beta work?

During private beta, access is limited. Join the waitlist and we'll notify you when a spot opens. Beta users get 50% off for life!
